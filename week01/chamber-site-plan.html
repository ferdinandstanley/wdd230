Lagos Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Selected Name: Lagos Chamber of Commerce

Reason: The name reflects the focus on the city of Lagos and its role in promoting business and community engagement. The inclusion of "Chamber" highlights the site's primary purpose of connecting businesses and fostering economic growth.

Site Purpose

  • To serve as a central hub for business resources, networking opportunities, and community events in Lagos.
  • To promote economic growth by showcasing local businesses, events, and regional data to both residents and visitors.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events can I attend to network with other businesses?
  • How can I become a member of the Lagos Chamber of Commerce?
  • What resources are available to help start a new business in Lagos?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0044cc (Blue) - Used for headers, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons.

Secondary Color: #f2a900 (Gold) - Used for accents, links, and background highlights.

Typography

Font 1: Roboto (sans-serif) - Used for body text for its readability.

Font 2: Merriweather (serif) - Used for headings to add a professional and elegant touch.
